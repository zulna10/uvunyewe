# Additional MPX

In order to receive additional MPX test coins, you can convert your XFI to MPX in mainnet.

<Steps>

### Step 1.

At the moment XFI is traded on two crypto currency exchanges, paired with USDT:

https://www.mexc.com/

- Minimum amount to purchase: 5 USDT
- Minimum amount to withdraw: 4 XFI
- Commission fee: 2 XFI

https://www.htx.com/

- Minimum amount to purchase: 10 USDT
- Minimum amount to withdraw: 1.5 XFI
- Commission fee: 0.5 XFI

Both of these exchange services have very simple registration processes, and you don’t need to verify your account to purchase and withdraw XFI.

In order to purchase XFI you need to deposit funds to your USDT wallet, then go to the spot trading page.

### Step 8.

Mainnet and testnet delegation processes are no different from each other. You can learn more about the delegation process when completing the “Bond” task on XFI TestPad.

### Step 9.

Mainnet and testnet XFI reward claiming processes are no different from each other.. You can learn more about the claiming process when completing the “Claim” task on XFI TestPad.

The XFI rewards you claimed in the mainnet can also be converted into MPX, in order to get more bonus MPX test coins.
